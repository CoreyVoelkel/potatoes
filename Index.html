Hot Chocolate

Needs

  • Water
  • Stove/Kettle
  • Hot Chocolate Mix

Directions

  1. Heat up Water until boiling or hot enough to not taste gross
  2. Once water is done heating up, fill up your special baby yoda mug
  3. Then you can take your Hot Chocolate Mix and mix it up. you can do half a scoop or double scoops, it doesnt really matter as long as your happy with it.
